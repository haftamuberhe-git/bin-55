Bin Guide

What goes where (from your leaflet).

Food / Pink lid (Weekly)

Weekly

Put food waste in your food caddy / pink-lidded bin:

  • All cooked and uncooked food
  • Fruit & vegetables (and peelings)
  • Teabags
  • Meat, fish, and bones
  • Garden waste (as shown on leaflet)
Use compostable liners provided. No plastic bags.

Blue bin (Fortnightly)

Every 2 weeks

Paper and cardboard only:

  • Cardboard cartons
  • Mail / envelopes
  • Newspapers
  • Cardboard
  • Magazines & brochures
  • Mixed paper
No plastic bags.

Brown bin (Fortnightly)

Every 2 weeks

Recycling items (as shown on leaflet):

  • Plastic pots, tubs, and trays
  • Glass bottles and jars (no lids)
  • Aerosols
  • Plastic bottles (all types)
  • Cans and tins
  • Foil and foil trays
No plastic bags.

Black bin (Three-weekly)

Every 3 weeks

Waste you can‚Äôt recycle:

  • Plastic bags (or recycle them at supermarkets)
  • Disposable nappies (fold and tie in a bag)
  • Bagged waste you can‚Äôt recycle
  • Pet waste (put in a tied bag)
All waste must be inside the bin. Lid closed. No extra bags at the side.
